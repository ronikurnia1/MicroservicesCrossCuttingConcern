

1.  Make sure docker-compose for Elasticsearch and Kibana already up and running
2.  Debug the app
3.  Open Kibana using your browser and open the following url: http://localhost:5601
4.  Click on "Explore on my own" link
5.  Configure Index patterns by clicking on "Connect to your Elasticsearch index"
6.  Click on "Create index pattern"
7.  Type globoticket* on Index pattern name
8.  Click on "Next step >" button
9.  Select @timestamp for Time field and click on "Create index pattern" button
10. Open humberger menu and select "Discover"
11. Explore the log
